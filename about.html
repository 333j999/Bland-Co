How we work

Three things we won't budge on.

The same standards on a £200 chain as on a five-figure watch.

01

We tell you the buy price and the resale price.

If we're buying from you, you'll see what we'd offer and roughly what we'd ask for it on the shelf. Same numbers we use internally. No mystery.

02

We don't list anything we can't authenticate.

If a watch is missing papers and we can't verify the movement, it doesn't go in the case. If gold isn't acid-tested and weighed in front of you, we don't quote on it.

03

When you ring, you speak to us.

No call queue, no chatbot, no junior reading from a script. The number on the contact page is the number on the bench.

Got a piece in mind?

Send a photo, ring the bench, or pop by between 10 and 6.
