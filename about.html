Who We Are

London's Premier

Bland & Co Jewellers was founded on a simple principle: that buying and selling luxury assets should be transparent, trustworthy, and genuinely rewarding. We are specialists in pre-owned luxury watches, precious metals, and bespoke jewellery.

Operating from Hatton Garden — the heart of London's fine jewellery trade for over three centuries — we combine deep market knowledge with a personal approach that larger dealers simply cannot offer.

Every piece we handle is rigorously authenticated. Every transaction is conducted with complete discretion. And every client leaves knowing they've received honest counsel from experts who genuinely care.

Our Location

The Heart of
Hatton Garden

Hatton Garden has been London's jewellery quarter for over three hundred years. Home to more diamond dealers per square mile than anywhere outside of Antwerp, it is a place built entirely on craft, trust, and expertise.

It is where we belong. Our presence here is not incidental — it is a statement of intent. When you work with Bland & Co, you are dealing with specialists embedded in the trade, with access to networks and knowledge that others simply don't have.

Expertise

With decades of combined experience across watchmaking, precious metals, and gemstones, our team brings genuine knowledge to every single transaction.
