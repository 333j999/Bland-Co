Authentication

We open it up. Movement signature, case finishing, dial printing, hands, crown, caseback, lume — checked against the reference. Serial number cross-referenced where we can. For higher-value pieces we'll often pull a second opinion from a specialist on the street.

Selling & Valuations

Two routes. Send photos and details through the valuation form for a same-day quote, or bring the piece in and we'll check condition, papers, and movement on the spot.

Safety & Security

Hatton Garden is one of the most heavily-traded jewellery streets in the world — security cameras everywhere, the trade is used to high-value pieces moving in and out. Booking ahead means we'll be ready and you won't be sat in reception.

We keep what we need for the transaction (and what UK AML rules require us to keep). Nothing gets shared with third parties. Ask us to delete your details and we will, subject to those legal retention windows.

Yes — registered in England and Wales, signed up to UK AML supervision, full Consumer Rights Act compliance. On higher-value deals we'll need to take a copy of your ID. That's the law, not us being awkward.
