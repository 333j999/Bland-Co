Our Process

Every piece that passes through Bland & Co undergoes a rigorous authentication process conducted by our in-house specialists. We cross-reference serial numbers, inspect movement signatures, verify hallmarks, and where applicable review original documentation. No item is listed or purchased without a signed-off authentication report.

Valuations

Our valuations are grounded in live market data, recent auction results, and decades of specialist knowledge across watches, jewellery, and precious metals.
